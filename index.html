Biography

I am a Research Scientist at ObjectVideo Labs working on computer vision and deep learning.

I obtained my Ph.D degree in Electrical Engineering from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Kansas in 2019. My advisor is Prof. Guanghui Wang. I was also affiliated with the Information and Telecommunication Technology Center (ITTC). I obtained my Master degree from Tufts University.
