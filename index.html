About me

I am a final year PhD candidate in the department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at The University of Kansas. I work with Prof. Guanghui (Richard) Wang. My research interests include computer vision and deep learning. On the theoretical side, I am interested in the optimization algorithms of deep learning. On the application side, my recent work is focusing on image and video understanding, including object detection and activity recognition.


I received my master from Tufts University in 2015. Before coming to US, I received my master from Graduate University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2012, and my B.S. in physics from Hunan Normal University in 2009.


I am actively looking for full time job on Computer Vision and Deep Learning.
