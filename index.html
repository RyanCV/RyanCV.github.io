About

I am a research scientist in ObjectVideo Labs at Alarm.com, working on image understanding and video analytics for Smart Home using computer vision and machine learning techniques.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from The University of Kansas, advised by Professor Guanghui Wang in 2019. Before that, I received my Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from Tufts University. My research interests mainly focus on the area of computer vision and deep learning, particularly on efficient learning of deep models for Human Pose Estimation, Face Recognition, 3D point cloud analysis, object detection and tracking.
