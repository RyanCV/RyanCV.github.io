About me

I am a PhD candidate in Electrical Engineer and Computer Science department at The University of Kansas from 2015. I work with Professor Richard Wang. My research interests include computer vision and deep learning.

I received my M. S. from Tufts University in 2015. Before coming to US, I received my master's degree from Graduate University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2012, advised by Professor Shouguo Wang, and my B. S. in physics from Hunan Normal University in 2009.
