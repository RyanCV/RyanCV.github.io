About

I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon. My current research interests include large language models (LLMs), multimodal AI systems, and intelligent agents.

Before that, I was a research scientist in ObjectVideo Labs at Alarm.com, working on image understanding and video analytics for Smart Home using computer vision and machine learning techniques.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from The University of Kansas, advised by Professor Guanghui Wang in 2019. Before that, I received my Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from Tufts University. My research focuses on computer vision and deep learning, particularly efficient learning for object detection, tracking, face recognition, and 3D point cloud analysis.
